Skimlinks helps support Feeder

Skimlinks is a service that helps support Feeder by adding affiliate links to posts that link to products and services.

Our donations are awesome. But they aren't enough to support the continued development of Feeder.

Skimlinks adds a little affiliate code to outbound links that link to products and services. If you purchase a product or service from that link, we get a percentage of the purchase total.

How does this affect you? It doesn't. You get your feeds and posts, and we transparently get a share of any purchases you make from Feeder.

Who loses? Nobody! It's fair for everyone invovled. You, as a user, don't notice anything different, and we get paid for our service.

Awesome! Right?

If you choose to disable this: We don't get anything. So we please urge you, if you for any reason choose to disable this feature, please donate to support us behind Feeder.
